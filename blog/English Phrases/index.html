bump intosb/sthvi. vt. 猛碰，撞to hit or knock against sth

  • She stepped backward and bumped into someone.（她踉跄后退，结果撞到了别人。）——权力的游戏 有声书
  • If I move slowly, I won’t bump into things.（如果我走得慢点，就不会撞到东西了。）——小猪佩奇 第一季

bump against/onsthvi. vt. 猛碰，撞to hit or knock against sth

  • I don’t want to bump her head against the door, you know, pinch her with the seat belt.（我不想又把她头撞到门上了，或者说，绑安全带的时候夹到她。）——摩登家庭 第一季
  • Remember the time you fell off your bike or bumped your head on a sharp corner?（还记得你从自行车上摔下或一头撞到尖角之时吗？）——TED-Ed

interact withsbvi. 相互交流，相互沟通when people interact with each other or interact, they communicate as they work or spend time together

  • Is he the only person you interact with?（他是唯一和你有交流的人吗？）——摩登家庭 第一季
  • Number 6: he never interacts with youon social media.（第六点，他从来不在社交媒体上和你互动。）——生活百科
  • He doesn’t interact much with others. He keeps to himself a lot.（他不怎么和其他人交流，有什么事都藏在心里。）——贝茨旅馆 第一季

interact withsthvi. 相互交流，相互沟通to act on or have an effect on each other

  • Still, it is just a story of how electrons interact with light.（然而，光与电子的相互作用仅仅是一个故事。） ——VOA 慢速-自然探索
  • It’s all about how one substance interacts with another to give us something new.（其本质在于两种物质发生化学反应从而生成新物质。）——化学公开课
  • And this looks at how energy interacts with electrons in atoms.（这能检测能量与原子中电子间的相互作用。）——化学公开课

interaction with/betweensbn. 交流，沟通the activity of talking to other people, and the way that people react to each other

  • He needs social interaction with his peers.（他需要和同龄人进行社交。） ——无耻家庭 美版 第六季
  • And was that your first interaction with Frank since he got back?（他回来后这是你第一次见他吗?）——曼哈顿计划 第二季
  • Their biggest concern: limited interaction between teachers and students.（他们最大的担忧是，师生之间受限制的互动。）——科学美国人-60秒科学

interaction with/betweensb/sthn. 相互影响the process by which two or more affect each other or change each other

  • What was your interaction with the environment?（你与周围的生活环境是如何相互影响的?） ——长生不老
  • Inside, it’s no more than the harmonious interaction between human and nature.（说穿了，无非是人与天地万物之间的和谐相处。）——舌尖上的中国
  • The interaction of the chemicals in the poison is not predictable.（毒药中化合物间的相互作用不可预测。）——迷离档案 危机边缘 第四季

in addition(to sb/sth)IDM. 此外；除…以外used when you want to introduce an additional point or relevant fact

  • Please submit your supporting documents. In addition, you will be required to pay an application fee.（请提交你的证明文件。此外，你将需要支付申请费。） ——你问我答
  • I can sing, too, you know, in addition to the ice skating.（除了滑冰，我还会唱歌。）——女子监狱 第一季
  • In addition to being a boss, I am also a waitress.（我除了是老板之外，还是一名服务员。）——破产姐妹 第二季

the addition ofsthn. 增添，添加the act of adding one thing to another

  • The addition of a child would disrupt even the happiest of couples.（有了孩子，就算最幸福的伴侣也会受到影响。） ——犯罪心理 第十季
  • The British Empire expanded rapidly with the addition of Borneo, Burma and New Guinea.（随着婆罗洲、缅甸和新几内亚的加入，大英帝国迅速扩张。）——黑猫系列读物
  • However, the addition of water to the land or soil may cause problems in some areas.（然而在有的地区，田地里水流的添加会造成问题。）——VOA慢速-农业报道

advance(on sth)n1. 预付款money which is paid to sb before the usual time of payment

  • I need an advance on my pay.（我需要提前预支些钱。） ——亲密治疗
  • I had to give Howie an advance on his allowance.（我只好让华仔预支他的零用钱。）——生活大爆炸 第七季

n2. （价格、价值的）上涨，上升a rise in the price, value, of sth

  • Any advance on 22’000? Anyone?（还有比22000更高的出价吗?还有吗?） ——碟中谍5
  • Chumhum has agreed to an advance on our fees. It was Cary’s case.（Chumhum网已经同意提高代理费。这是Cary的案子。）——傲骨贤妻 第六季

a advantage oversbn. 有利条件，优势something that puts you in a better position than other people

  • What gives you a distinct advantage over all the other capable people here?（相比其他有能力的人，你有什么明显的优势？） ——亿万 第二季
  • You were always the better engineer. But I do have one advantage over you.（你当工程师一直比我高明，但我确实有一项优势。）——疑犯追踪 第二季

take advantage ofsthIDM. 利用if you take advantage of sth, you make good use of it while you can

  • Glad to see you’re taking advantage of the opportunities offered.（很高兴看到你利用了提供给你的机会。）——英美剧集
  • Just taking advantage of the local resources.（我只是在利用本地资源。）——神秘博士 第七季
  • Harry took advantage of the temporary silence to eat more breakfast.（哈利利用这短暂的沉默，加紧吃他的早餐。）——哈利波特与混血王子

take advantage ofsbIDM. 占…的便宜；欺骗；捉弄if sb takes advantage of you, they treat you unfairly for their own benefit

  • Frank’s just too soft hearted, and people take advantage of him.（弗兰克就是心太软了，人们利用他这一点来占他的便宜。） ——《飘》
  • He thinks he can take advantage of you because you’re a woman.（他觉得你是女人所以就可以占你的便宜。）——我为喜剧狂 第三季
  • I went down to the free clinic and I took full advantage of the “free” part for you.（我去了免费诊所，并为你大大占了它“免费”的便宜。）——衰女翻身 第一季

be/work toone’sadvantageIDM. 对…有利to give you an advantage; (to be) better for you

  • It’ll definitely be to our advantage to have someone who can go in and out freely.（有人可以自由进出对我们来说绝对是优势。） ——死亡笔记 英文配音版
  • I used to swim in college — the one place being tall actually worked to my advantage.（我上大学的时候经常游泳，在大学个子高对我来说很有利。）——英美剧集

to good/best advantageIDM. 最有利地；最有效地；使优点突出in a way that shows the best features of sth

  • It showed to advantage the good shape of his figure.（这套衣服凸显了他的好身材。） ——还乡
  • There his faculties stagnate — they cannot develop or appear to advantage.（在这里他的才能会停滞不前，难以提升或有效施展出来。）——简 · 爱（原著）

use/turnsthtoone’sadvantageIDM. 最有利地；最有效地；使优点突出to use sth in order to benefit from it, especially when it might be expected to harm or damage you

  • I’m going to use the rules of the Death Note to my advantage.（我打算利用死亡笔记的规则。） ——死亡笔记 英文配音版
  • I saw an opportunity to turn things to my advantage, and I did.（我看到有机会可以把事情转变成对我有利的条件，我就利用了这个机会。）——闪电侠 第一季

an alternative tosthn. 可供选择的事物；可供替代的事物sth that can be chosen instead of sth else

  • Is there no alternative to retreat then?（除了撤退，没有别的路可以选了吗?） ——罗马 第一季
  • The only alternative to being taken prisoner was to die fighting.（不想当俘虏，唯一的选择就是誓死而战。）——淘金式巧攻大学英语词汇

adapt(oneself)tosthvi. vt. 适应to change one’s ideas or behavior in order to deal with a new situation successfully

  • Some animals adapt to new surroundings.（一些动物会适应新的环境。） ——唐顿庄园 第三季
  • I guess he had a lot on his mind trying to adapt to the life on the inside.（我猜他在想办法适应牢里的生活。） ——肖申克的救赎
  • Look, it’s an AI, an artificial intelligence. It can think for itself, adapt itself to new situations.（看，这是一个 A，人工智能。它本身能够自主思考，使自身适应新的情境。。）——星际之门亚特兰蒂斯 第二季
  • When you go to a foreign country, you must adapt yourself to new manners and customs.（你去外国时，必须使自己适应新的风俗习惯。） ——刘毅词汇5000
